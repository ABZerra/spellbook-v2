# Example Input: Discovery Plan

We want to introduce AI-assisted ticket triage for support teams. Unknowns include expected accuracy, compliance constraints for data handling, and whether agents trust automated prioritization.

Evidence synthesis input:
- Research question: what prevents support teams from trusting assisted ticket prioritization?
- Converging themes: teams want a rationale they can audit, and they fear false urgency more than missing low-priority work.
- Contradictions: newer agents want stronger automation; experienced agents want override control first.
- Gaps: no compliance answer yet on what ticket data can be sent to models.

Primary JTBD:
- When support teams triage a new queue of incoming tickets, they want reliable priority guidance, so they can focus attention on the most urgent cases without re-sorting everything manually.
